Our Branches

NKCyber is a student-run organization at Northern Kentucky University. Our goal is to help everyone learn and experience key concepts of computing and cybersecurity. Along our goal is to give you more hands-on experience in areas of cybersecurity that may not be touched on in class and provide supplemental knowledge to classroom experience.

We accomplish this by providing free hands-on training and lessons at NKU, participating in competitions like BearcatCTF and CCDC, travelling to conferences and events like STLP and TSA, and so much more. NKCyber is made up of multiple divisions:

Cyber Security Training

The Cyber Security Training Division is our Weekly meetings during the academic year. These meetings consist of Cyber Security Labs setup by students, Guest Lecturers, Capture the Flag Competitions, or Competition Preparation.

Research and Development

Discover the junction of coding and cybersecurity in R&D. Join a close-knit community that sparks innovation through impactful projects. Step into the realm of coding, unlocking your cyber potential.

Build Team

Get hands-on with infrastructure, networking, and DevOps in the Build Team. We keep things running and teach secure server setups. Join our close community for learning and cyber empowerment.
